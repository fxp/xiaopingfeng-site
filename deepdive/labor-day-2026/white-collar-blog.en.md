## Next Steps for Three Types of People

**Working professionals**: Redefine yourself as a "systems architect + AI agent orchestrator," not a "code author / report writer." **The 1–5 year window is what Amodei is giving us — but the real timeline may be even shorter.**

**Business leaders**: You must proactively design **new talent succession mechanisms** — if AI is taking over entry-level tasks, how do people accumulate tacit knowledge early in their careers? This is the most underestimated HR challenge of 2026–2028.

**Policymakers**: Traditional models of unemployment relief and retraining don't fit this situation. **Large-scale white-collar retraining requires a new framework** — one that is neither the "skills swap" approach used in manufacturing transitions, nor the "change majors" solution the education system can offer.
